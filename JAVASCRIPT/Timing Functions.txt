TIMING FUNCTIONS

1.setTimeout()
-Runs a Function once after a delay in (in milliseconds)

Syntax:
setTimeout(function,delay);

Example:
console.log("Start");
setTimeout(()=>{
    console.log("This runs After 3 seconds (Once.)")
})

2.setInterval(function,interval)
Example:
let count=1;
let timer=setInterval(()=>{
    console.log("This runs for Evey three Seconds")
})


TIMEOUT Function

clearInterval(id);
clearTimeout(id)

SHORT NOTES
-setTimeout ->Run once after delay

-setInterval ->Run repeatedly After delay

-clearTimeout ->Stops setTimeout
-clearInterval ->Stops setInterval

Both are Async FUNCTIONS
